Course Objectives and Overview 
Introduce the method and examples 
 of basic concepts of sociology, important theories, sociological analysis and interpretation of social issues. Through this, understand modern Korean society
by illuminating sociological phenomena and problems from a sociological perspective and utilizing and analyzing sociological concepts, 
 in order to cultivate problem solving skills.
Prerequisite (Prerequisite Learning) 
none
Course operation method 
Professor lectures, student presentations and discussions
Grading Method 
Final Exam (25%), Page (45%), Class Participation (30%), 

* Participation in class: attendance, participation in YSEC, presentation and discussion, and other contributions

+  is given as a teaching capacity point according to the degree of effort of all students

Final grading is based on 1) sincere class participation and serious academic attitudes, and 2) basic concepts of sociology.
A good understanding of science and theories, and 3) the importance of insight into social phenomena and problem-solving skills.
