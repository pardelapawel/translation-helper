Course Objectives and Overview 
This course called <Reading Contemporary Art> 
The art side of art works, not a skimming class of art history in general
Digging into the relationship between art and society based on our hidden background and understanding of social situations
I want to be evil.
The museum explores our contemporary society and the field of contemporary art that takes place in it.
I want to understand the world.
The purpose of this study is to diagnose the appearance of modern society through various art phenomena.
In other words, we can learn how to read and read our paintings.
Furthermore, I would like to point out social issues drawn from art across the ages and spaces. 
In the main issue called Art Issues, the relationship between art and society 
Identify and understand the examples you find.
It's not just a class for appreciation of art works, so the 'social issues' 
In order to grasp the issue's correlation and social background,
The phrases must be parallel.
The main task of this course is to explore the field of contemporary art.
Interview exhibitions and artists' works that can be viewed directly by team projects

Summary		 
This Class is a survey coures of Contempory Art and Contempory times.
It covers Arts of western, estern and Korea.
The three main goals of this class are 
1.Help students to understand the overall structure of contemporary art world
2. Help students to get the knowledge about the movements in contemporary 
art scene (from Modernism, more focus on the period of the 2nd World War)
3.Help students to have their own view and taste for contemporary art through 
the field research (to real art world in Korea)
