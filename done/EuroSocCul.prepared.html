				Course Target	
				
All students interested in European society and culture.
		
		
			
		

				Course Objectives and Overview	
				
Modern Europe is undergoing a transnational integration project that is unprecedented in human history. 
It is a land of attraction where history meets the future. This course is based on the splendid history and cultural heritage.
European social spheres still exerting great influence on the world's topographical maps
Its primary purpose is to analyze groups and cultural phenomena. This course is particularly focused on 'Europe of Culture'
Focusing on various perspectives on European cultural competitiveness and European Union's cultural policy. 
I would like to use it for consideration.
