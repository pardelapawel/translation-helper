				Course Target	
				
Yonsei University Students
		
		
			
		

				Course Objectives and Overview	
				
The purpose of this course is to study to understand international politics in the era of globalization
in theory and with the case studies, understanding the phenomenon of globalization
is occurring not only in the realm of Economics, but also in the environment, security, labor, etc.
