Summary		 
This is a small group (max. 12 students) seminar course designed for freshmen 
who share interest in, but not necessarily knowledge of, Russian culture. Under
the guidance of Russian specialists (Korean & Russian faculty members) students 
will explore various aspects of Russian culture, from Russian tea and vodka to 
Russian modern theater. Pass / Fail course.
