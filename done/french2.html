Course Target 
Anyone who has completed the Basic (Basic) level of French is available. 
Intermediate students are recommended to take advanced French courses offered by our department.)
Course Objectives and Overview 
This lecture is about the level of FESTIVAL (1) used in French (1). 
This course is based on the grammar and vocabulary learned in basic French.
To explain systematic grammar through practical colloquial learning and interpretation of written sentences
Intermediate learns conversation, sentence reading, listening, writing and understanding of French culture 
Aimed at the level of integrated instruction.
Prerequisite (Prerequisite Learning) 
French (1) Textbook FESTIVAL (1), or equivalent French (1) or Humanities France
Have taken basic language (1) or have studied basic grammar in high school or other language
First, the level of learning the complex past).
Course operation method 
The main textbook, FESTIVAL (2), focuses on listening skills by utilizing related audiovisual materials.
To improve application skills through grammar exercises and other related multimedia materials.
Sun a practical approach to language and culture. To this end, through active participation of students
Aim at practical language acquisition.
