				Course Target	
				
In-depth my understanding of French culture and art, especially high-level culture such as literature, philosophy, painting and architecture
A student who wants to learn dragons. Enrich your life with new appreciation for culture and arts
Any student who wants to enjoy relaxing and leisurely.
		
		
			
		

				Course Objectives and Overview	
				
A variety of literary, philosophical, painting and architectural concepts that a respected cultured person in Western society should know.
This course is designed to help students gain high-level professional knowledge easily and for a semester. Scoop
A must-have class before you travel in France !! To learn piecemeal knowledge
It's not just about the idea of 
Another goal. After a semester class, I appreciate architecture, painting, and literature.
Incredible skills and new perspectives on historical philosophy 
Newly educated men will be born.
