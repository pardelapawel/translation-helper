Course Target 
All undergraduate students (excluding science and engineering majors)
Course Objectives and Overview 
Introduce modern astronomical answers to fundamental questions about the universe at the level of university liberal arts.

-The life and death of stars: various stars and what they say
-Origin of the Universe: The discovery of cosmic expansion and cosmic explosion
Prerequisite (Prerequisite Learning) 
none
Course operation method 
-Class proceeds with writing and explanation 
-Use appropriate presentations, media such as documentaries and movies according to the lecture topic 
-It is possible to submit questions and opinions by using cyber class space. 
-You can actively participate in lectures through presentations and hands-on help activities.
Grading Method 
Class Participation: 30% (Attendance, Practice, Report)
Final Exam: 70%
