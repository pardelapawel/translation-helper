summary

  Computer algorithms are one of the core subjects of computer science. Good software requires good data structures and proper algorithms. Thus, knowledge of algorithms is fundamental and necessary in all areas of computer. Therefore, in this course, students will learn how to design and analyze algorithms related to important sorting, searching, graphing, string processing, and data compression. This process builds on the basics of what is a good algorithm and how to make a good algorithm. Deep mathematical knowledge is not required, but it requires systematic, logical, and analytical thinking, so it is necessary to study while thinking about why.
