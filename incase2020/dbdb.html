summary

  Unlike the past, where decisions were made based on intuition or experience, modern society uses information technology to make decisions based on big data. Therefore, the ability to efficiently find the necessary information and recreate it with new information is becoming increasingly important. As a result, a database system that is an information technology for efficient storage, retrieval, and management of data is recognized as an important element technology.
“Database system” covers various theories and techniques used in databases, such as database modeling, database language (SQL), indexing, transaction and concurrency control, backup and restore, to understand the structure of data and to effectively store and operate the data.
