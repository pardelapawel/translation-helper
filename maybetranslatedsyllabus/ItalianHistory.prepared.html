				Course Objectives and Overview	
				
One of the important sources of Western civilization, Italy, from ancient times to today 
Through its long history, it has contributed to the development of human civilization. Especially in the center of the Mediterranean
It can function as a bridge connecting the cultures of the East, West, Mediterranean, Africa and inland Europe.
This geographic location is simply one isolated specific to Italian history. 
It played an important role in making it part of world history, not local history. 
From this point of view, this class covers the Italian Peninsula and Sicily from ancient to modern times.
Analyze a series of historical processes that took place around Sardinia and Sardinia, 
It aims to grasp the meaning of world history.
