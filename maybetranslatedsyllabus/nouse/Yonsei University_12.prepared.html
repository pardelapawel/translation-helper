Course Objectives and Overview 
The power structure is an institutional device for the realization of national sovereignty and guarantee of fundamental rights. Thus in Korea
In discussing power structures, apart from sectarianism, objective and academic analysis is attempted. 
It is very important to come up with alternatives. In this lesson,
I'll cover in depth.
