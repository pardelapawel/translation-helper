Course Target 
For students who are new to French.
Foreign Language High school student from French department or lived in French
Students are encouraged to take French 2,3.
Course Objectives and Overview 
-This class is for students who are new to French. 
-Beginning with the French alphabet and pronunciation exercises, get a basic knowledge of French. 

-In addition, students learn various vocabulary and expressions and can speak in real situations.
Prerequisite (Prerequisite Learning) 
none.
Course operation method 
-Lectures will be held centering on Textbook Festival 1.
The text and culture sections will be selectively taught. 
-This is for students who are learning French for the first time.
Repetitive exercises will continue to be assigned to the assignment and will be checked.  
-Practice your French reading using audiovisual materials to the extent that time permits,
Opportunities to experience French culture
