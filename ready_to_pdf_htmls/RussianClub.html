Summary		 
This is a small group (max. 12 students) seminar course designed for freshmenwho share interest in, but not necessarily knowledge of, Russian culture.Underthe guidance of Russian specialists (Korean & Russian faculty members) studentswill explore various aspects of Russian culture, from Russian tea and vodka toRussian modern theater.Pass / Fail course.
